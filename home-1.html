About Me:
Emanuele CIRRONIS

Graduated in video games from ESMA Montpellier. My academic background and technical skills have given me a versatile profile, but it's programming that really sparks my passion.My course at ESMA gave me a generalist education in video games, enabling me to master creative tools such as Maya and Zbrush for 3D modeling and animation, as well as texturing skills with Photoshop, Substance Designer and Painter.

However, it's in programming that I find my true fulfillment. Eager to specialize in this field, I chose to focus my efforts on learning and mastering the Unity and Unreal Engine game engines and their languages during my 3 years at school.

As an active member of various group projects (Horror Game Project, JAM, 3rd Year Project, etc.), I learned how to work effectively with graphic designers, designers and other programmers, through the use of project management software such as Github and Youtrack. My experience as a programmer in a group has enabled me to understand the importance of communication and sharing skills to achieve a common goal. My professional project is to become a Gameplay Programmer. I'd like to design and develop a variety of game mechanisms, capable of offering a fun and captivating experience.
